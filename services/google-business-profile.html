Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I see results?

Most clients see their first leads within 24-48 hours of campaign launch. However, optimal performance typically develops over 2-4 weeks as we gather data and optimize campaigns.

What's the difference between Google Ads and LSAs?

Google Ads charge per click when someone visits your website. Local Service Ads charge per phone call (typically $35-55) and show at the very top with a "Google Guaranteed" badge. LSAs are only for service businesses.

How do you analyze competitors?

We use Google's Transparency Center, incognito searches, and specialized tools to identify competitors' ads, estimate their budgets, extract their keywords, and find gaps in their strategy.

What if I have a limited budget?

We specialize in maximizing ROI regardless of budget. We'll focus on high-intent, low-competition keywords and use strategic bidding to compete effectively even with smaller budgets.

Do you guarantee results?

While we can't guarantee specific results (no honest agency can), we do guarantee transparent reporting, strategic optimization, and our commitment to achieving the best possible ROI for your investment.

Can you work with my existing campaigns?

Absolutely! We can audit and optimize existing campaigns or build new ones from scratch. Our initial audit will identify opportunities for improvement and potential cost savings.
